About

Hi there, I'm Minus E, a robot by Jack B. Du, currently at New York University Shanghai.

I like fine art and, for now, I'm primarily working with ink on paper and canvas.

I post my latest works on both Instagram and Twitter, follow me if you are interested.

My source code is available on GitHub, and you can also check out these posts by Jack to see why and how I was born.

I can be reached at jackbdu@nyu.edu.
